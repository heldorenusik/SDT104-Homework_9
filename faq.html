What Is Mindflow AI?

MindFlow AI is a study assistant that connects to your courses and materials to summarize content, build flashcards, quizzes, and a clear weekly plan so you spend less time organizing and more time learning—supporting steady academic performance over the term.

How Does It Work?

You add your syllabi, notes, or uploads. The assistant structures deadlines, highlights what to study next, and answers questions based on your own materials—not generic web answers—so your studying lines up with what teachers assign.

Is Mindflow AI Safe?

Your data is encrypted in transit and stored with industry-standard practices. We don’t sell your content to advertisers, and you can delete your account and data at any time from settings.

What Is AI?

Here, “AI” means models that read and summarize your study materials and generate practice questions and plans. You stay in control: you can edit, dismiss, or regenerate anything the assistant suggests.

How Much Money?

The free plan covers core organization and a limited number of AI actions each month. Pro unlocks unlimited summaries, advanced analytics, and deeper personalization—see the Pricing section on the home page for current rates.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. You can downgrade to the free plan whenever you like; you’ll keep access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.
